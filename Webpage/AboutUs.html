About Us

Voyager is a trusted community marketplace for people to list, discover, and book unique accommodations around the world -online or from a mobile phone or tablet.

Whether an apartment for a night, a castle for a week, or a villa for a month, Voyager connects people to unique travel experiences, at any price point, in more than 34,000 cities and 190 countries. And with world-class customer service and a growing community of users, Voyager is the easiest way for people to monetize their extra space and showcase it to an audience of millions.
